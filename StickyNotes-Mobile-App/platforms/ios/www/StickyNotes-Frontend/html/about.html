    Welcome to StickyNotes!

    StickyNotes is your go-to app for jotting down ideas, reminders, or anything that you need to remember. Created in 2023 by Son Nguyen, our app offers a user-friendly platform to make note-taking a breeze. Whether you're a student, a professional, or anyone in between, StickyNotes is designed to help you keep track of your day-to-day tasks with ease. Note that you can also take advantage of ALL features using a desktop!

    Notable Features

    • Create unlimited sticky notes for all your note-taking needs.
    • Access your notes from anywhere, anytime, even without an Internet connection!
    • Interactive drag-and-drop functionality to rearrange notes as you wish.
    • Pin your most important notes to the top for easy access.
    • Calendar integration for easy scheduling and event management.
    • Import and export options for seamless note sharing and backup.
    • Automatic saving to prevent data loss and ensure your notes are always up to date.
    • Advanced search feature to quickly find the notes you need.
    • Built-in calculator, mood tracker, and timer for added productivity.
    • Secure sign-in and account creation with our encrypted databases to keep your data safe.

    How It Works

    Using StickyNotes is straightforward. Click 'Add A Note' to start, type your note, and it automatically saves. Edit by clicking on a note and pressing the edit button, and delete any note with a simple click on the delete icon. Your notes stay on your screen until you decide to remove them.
